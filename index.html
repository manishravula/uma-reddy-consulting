About

Science that has walked the fields of Telangana

Dr. Ravula Uma Reddy serves as Associate Director of Research at the Regional Agricultural Research Station (RARS), Warangal — part of Professor Jayashankar Telangana State Agricultural University (PJTSAU), the state's premier agricultural university. His career spans soil science, agronomy, and crop research across the crops that define Telangana farming: rice, maize, cotton, and redgram.

He has led zonal research and extension advisory councils, cotton and paddy research schemes, residential training programs in modern agriculture and agri-business, and seed distribution drives for farming communities — including dedicated programs for tribal beneficiaries. For over a decade his advice has reached lakhs of farmers through television and radio — HMTV, V6 News, T News, ETV, 6TV, All India Radio, and PJTSAU's own farm broadcasts.

Through this practice, that experience is available directly: to a farmer planning a season, an entrepreneur building an agri-venture, or an institution seeking a research and training partner.
